Framing a New Home

These are pictures from our project to take a house to Covington LA. We built the walls for the Owens Family. We had help from Lumber King, Alton Blakely Ford and Gator Made trailers. We used the old Alton Blakely Water Sports building to put the walls up. Thanks to our volunteers for all the help!

The Owens Family

The Owen Family is moving from their one bedroom apartment to this 4-bedroom house built in Somerset and transported by truck and trailer donated by Alton Blakley Ford and Gator Made Trailers. Lumber King also donated 50 studs to the cause. The house was finally erected in the last week of October 2011!

Our Young Fundraisers

Somerset Christian School hosted a fundraiser for Habitat for Humanity of Pulaski County by taking home house shaped banks and filling them with pocket change. In total, the students raised $889.42! Mrs. Phelps fourth grade class raised the most money for Habitat for Humanity and was rewarded with an ice cream party!

The Genesis House

The Genesis House is the current name of our first house. We anticipate building the home in the Summer of 2012. We will need a total of $55,000 to complete this home. Due to our fundraising efforts, we now have $21,000 in the bank, which means we are in need of $34,000 more. We would love to be able to add your name to our donor list! We plan on building the house so it is energy efficient and "green". It will have 3 to 4 bedrooms depending on the size of the selected family. The family will have a mortgage on the house and provide "sweat equity" in order to build it. Volunteers are used to reduce the cost of the house to make it more affordable to the selected family.

We would like to welcome you go join us in making the Genesis House a reality! There are currently several volunteer opportunities available. We need your help on the resource development committee as we continue to raise money for the first build. You could also join the family selection committee and assist us in our selection of a family with whom to partner. We can always use a hand in the construction of the new home as well. You do not have to have experience in any of these fields to work with Habitat! Please contact us for additional information regarding these opportunities!
