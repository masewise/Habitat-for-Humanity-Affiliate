Run Home for Habitat 5K

Date: Saturday, September 29, 2012
Time: Registration from 7:00 - 7:45AM, Race begins at 8:00AM
Location:
Pisgah Presbyterian Church
Somerset, KY 42503
Directions:
Take Highway 27 South to 914 Bypass. Turn right (west) onto 914 Bypass at light #25. Take first left. Pisgah Church & Schoolhouse will be on the hill.
Registration Form: Run Home For Habitat 5k Registration Form
Entry Fee: $20 before September 24 / $25 after September 24 through race day
The Course:
Start at Pisgah Church, down Old Monticello Road through the Waitsboro Subdivision and loop back up Old Monticello Road. Left on Earl Neely Road and right to the Pisgah Church to the finish. There is an 80 foot elevation gain and loss on the course. The course will be open so be aware of automobiles on the course. There will be at least two water stops along the course.
T-Shirt and Packet Pick-Up:
All paid entrants will receive a t-shirt and goody bag. Day of race entrants will not be guaranteed these items. Packet pick-up starts at 7:00AM at the race starting site (Pisgah Presbyterian Church and Schoolhouse).
